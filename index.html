BRAND REPUTATION Established watch brands have a long-standing reputation for producing high-quality timepieces. Researching the brand's history, reputation, and customer reviews can help validate the authenticity of a watch.
QUALITY AND CRAFTSMANSHIP Genuine watches are known for their superior quality and meticulous craftsmanship. Pay attention to details like the weight, finishing, logo placement, and overall build quality. Counterfeit watches often have noticeable flaws or inconsistencies.
SERIAL NUMBERS Authentic watches typically have a unique serial number engraved on the case or between the lugs. These numbers can be verified with the brand to ensure their authenticity.
